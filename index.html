THE HEALTHY SIDE OF OLIVES

Does extra virgin matter?

We press olives cold. That's what "extra virgin" means. Because if you heat olives, you cook the personality out of them. And we like personality.

Cold pressing keeps all the good things olives develop under the Mediterranean sun. The natural antioxidants. The flavor. The character.

THE TASTE

Gently bitter, slightly spicy

Amarea is single origin. Early harvest. Cold-pressed. Milled shortly after picking.

On the palate? Gently bitter. Slightly spicy. Balanced. You'll notice a delicate olive aroma with hints of fresh grass and tomato leaves. And that little peppery kick at the end? That's not a flaw. That's character.
