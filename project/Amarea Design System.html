Olio Extra Vergine di Oliva · Design System v1

A confident, anti-elitist olive-oil brand. Forest-green ink, signature mint stripes, wide uppercase display type, and an almost-flat, square-cornered aesthetic. Tokens flow primitives → semantic so the real fonts and any tuning drop in one place.

01 — Primitives

Colour

One dominant green scale carries the whole brand — forest at the dark end (ink, buttons), mint in the middle (the stripe), pale tints at the light end. Neutrals lean warm. Olive-gold is a rare accent, mostly living in photography.

02 — Semantic tokens

Colour, by intent

Components never reference a raw primitive. They reach for intent — --color-ink, --button-bg, --badge-border — which maps to a primitive here. Re-point one line and the whole system follows.

01 — Primitives

Typography

Display: Monument Extended Black — wide, uppercase, set here with Archivo at full width + weight 900 as the swappable Google stand-in. Body: Founders Grotesk — shown with Hanken Grotesk. Override --font-display / --font-body to drop in the licensed faces.

01 — Primitives

Spacing & radius

A 4-based spacing scale keeps rhythm consistent. Radius is deliberately near-zero — the brand is square. Only the status pill and badges take a small 4px round.
